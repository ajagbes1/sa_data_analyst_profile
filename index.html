About Me

Why Hire Me?

I am a data analyst with a strong engineering background and a passion for transforming large datasets into actionable insights. Using tools like Excel, Power BI, and SQL, I uncover valuable trends and patterns that drive business growth and informed decision-making.
As a natural problem solver, I thrive on tackling complex challenges and developing innovative solutions to improve efficiency and streamline processes. My ability to think critically and approach problems with creativity has been instrumental in delivering impactful results.
In addition to my technical expertise, I have successfully led a team of talented data analysts on various projects. By fostering a collaborative and innovative environment, I empower team members to excel, enhancing both problem-solving and productivity.
Outside of data analysis, I channel my creativity into steel graphic design. This artistic pursuit not only brings me joy but also sharpens my ability to think outside the box and approach challenges with a fresh, unique perspective.

Business Intelligence Dashboard

I designed a user-friendly Business Intelligence (BI) dashboard that transformed raw operational and employee data into actionable insights through meaningful visualizations. By integrating key performance indicators (KPIs), trend analysis, and interactive elements, the dashboard provided a comprehensive view of workforce productivity, operational efficiency, and overall business performance. With a focus on real-time analytics, this tool empowered stakeholders to make data-driven decisions, identify opportunities for improvement, optimize resource allocation, and enhance strategic planning effectively.

  • Client: Fin-Tech
  • Date: August 2024
  • Service: Power-BI: Unlocking The Power of Employee Data for Organizational Insights.

Analysing Netflix Data for Business Insights

I conducted an in-depth analysis of Netflix's content and user engagement data, transforming raw datasets into actionable insights through advanced analytics and interactive visualizations. By examining key metrics such as viewership trends, content performance, and user retention rates, the analysis provided a comprehensive understanding of audience preferences and platform efficiency. Utilizing tools like trend analysis and predictive modeling, the project identified opportunities for content optimization, personalized recommendations, and strategic growth. This data-driven approach empowered stakeholders to enhance user satisfaction, improve content strategy, and drive long-term platform success.

  • Client: Netflix
  • Date: January 2025
  • Service: Netflix Data for Business Insights

Stock & Inventory Tracker

I developed an intuitive Stock and Inventory Tracker that streamlined the management of inventory data by converting it into actionable insights through dynamic dashboards and real-time analytics. By incorporating critical metrics such as stock levels, turnover rates, and supply chain performance, the tool provided a holistic view of inventory health and operational efficiency. With interactive features, the tracker enabled businesses to minimize stockouts, reduce overstocking, and optimize inventory turnover. Designed for ease of use, this solution empowered teams to make informed decisions, improve resource allocation, and enhance overall supply chain productivity.

  • Client: Christoye Enterprises
  • Date: September 2023
  • Service: Stock & Inventory Tracker
